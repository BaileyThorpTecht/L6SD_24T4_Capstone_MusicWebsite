FAQ

How can I share music legally on this website?

You require permission from the copyright holder to play the music on our website without fear of punishment.

How do I create a song?

Scroll to the bottom of the page and click "Add Song" then select the song in your "Song Playback" box, once selected simply use the Chord Section to choose from several predefined chords or use the fretboard at the top to add custom chords to the song then click "Add Chord" found at the bottom of the page to add the chord to your song.

How do I save a chord?

Either select a predefined chord using the "Chord Section" or input your own custom chord by manually using the fretboard, then under "Chord Saving" type a name for your chord and then click the "Save Chord" button to save a chord.

How do I create an account?

To create an account click the "Register" button in the top right corner of the main page, then fill in all the details for registration and remember to click links for both the "Terms and Conditions" and "Privacy Policy" to enable the ability to check the checkboxes, afterwards click Register.

What does chord saving do?

Chord saving allows you to save chords you either selected or manually inputted and allows you to save them to the "Chord Saving" list so you can always select the chord so the fretboard automatically changes it current chord to the one you selected this way you wont have to remember the string pattern for each chord you wanna remember.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password simply click the "Login" button in the top right corner of the main page and then click the "Forgot Password? Reset Password" link at the bottom, follow the link and then input your email into the box to send a "Reset My Password" request to your email address, then follow the steps displayed in the email to reset your password.
